Privacy Policy
Your Privacy

This app is designed for personal use by a small group of users and does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Data Access

The app displays images and associated text that are hosted online via Firebase Storage. These files are not user-generated and do not contain personally identifiable information.

No Tracking

This app does not include analytics, tracking, advertising, or third-party data collection of any kind.

Authentication

Anonymous authentication is used only to securely access images hosted in Firebase Storage. No user accounts, emails, or identifiers are stored or tracked.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact the app developer.
